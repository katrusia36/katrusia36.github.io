Our Gelato

Chocolat artisanal gelato is handmade fresh everyday by our gelato makers,
using premium quality ingredients.
We developed our own gelato recipes after years of work and research,
preserving and improving the Italian traditional flavors.

About us

Chocolat is the first italian gelateria dedicated to chocolate experience, founded in Milan in 2002.
Behind Chocolat there is a group of Italian gelato chefs and entrepreneurs,
with more than 60 years of experience in the artisanal gelato production.
